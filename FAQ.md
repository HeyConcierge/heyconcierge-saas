# HeyConcierge FAQ

## General Questions

### What is HeyConcierge?
HeyConcierge is an AI-powered guest communication platform for vacation rental hosts. It handles guest messages 24/7 via WhatsApp, provides instant answers to common questions, and sends automated check-in instructions with personalized arrival details.

### How does it work?
1. Connect your property calendar (Airbnb, VRBO, or any iCal feed)
2. Add your WhatsApp number to your listing
3. HeyConcierge automatically sends guests their check-in details and answers questions
4. You stay in control — review conversations and intervene anytime

### Is it really available 24/7?
Yes. HeyConcierge responds instantly to guest messages at any time, day or night. No more missing messages while you sleep or losing bookings because you didn't respond fast enough.

## Setup & Integration

### What platforms do you integrate with?
HeyConcierge works with any platform that provides an iCal calendar feed:
- Airbnb
- VRBO/HomeAway
- Booking.com
- Direct bookings (Google Calendar, iCloud)
- Property management systems (Guesty, Hostaway, etc.)

### How long does setup take?
Less than 5 minutes:
1. Paste your calendar link
2. Add your property details
3. Set your WhatsApp number
4. Done!

### Do I need a separate WhatsApp number?
We recommend using a dedicated business WhatsApp number, but you can use your personal number if preferred. WhatsApp Business API is not required.

### What information do I need to provide?
- Property address
- Check-in/check-out times
- WiFi password
- Access instructions (lockbox code, key location, etc.)
- House rules
- Any other info you want guests to have

## Features

### What questions can HeyConcierge answer?
Common guest questions like:
- Check-in time and instructions
- WiFi password
- House rules
- Local recommendations
- Parking information
- Appliance instructions
- Emergency contacts
- Check-out procedures

### Can I customize the responses?
Yes. You can customize all automated messages and responses to match your property and communication style.

### Does it send check-in instructions automatically?
Yes. When a new booking is detected, HeyConcierge automatically sends:
- Welcome message
- Check-in time and instructions
- WiFi details
- Important house rules
- Your contact info

You control exactly when these are sent (e.g., 24 hours before arrival).

### Can I see all conversations?
Yes. You have full visibility into every conversation through your dashboard. You can jump in anytime or let the AI handle it.

### What if the AI doesn't know the answer?
If HeyConcierge encounters a question it can't answer, it:
1. Notifies you immediately
2. Tells the guest you'll respond shortly
3. Learns from your response for next time
