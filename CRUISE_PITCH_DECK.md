# AI Destination Intelligence for Cruise Lines
## HeyConcierge Strategic Pivot — February 2026

---

## The Insight

**Every cruise ship answers the same 50 questions about every port.**

- "What should I do in Barcelona?"
- "Where's the best local restaurant?"  
- "How do I get to the Sagrada Familia?"
- "Is this tour worth it?"

**Problem:** Guest services teams are overwhelmed. Guests get generic answers or none at all.

**Solution:** One AI system that gives every passenger a personal destination expert in their native language.

---

## Market Opportunity

### Cruise Industry by the Numbers

- **~300 major cruise ships** operating globally
- **30 million passengers** annually (pre-pandemic: 32M)
- **Average 3,000 passengers** per ship per sailing
- **90+ ports of call** worldwide with regular cruise traffic

### Market Concentration

- **Top 3 operators control 75% of market:**
  - Carnival Corporation (9 brands, 92 ships)
  - Royal Caribbean (4 brands, 64 ships)  
  - Norwegian Cruise Line (3 brands, 30 ships)

**Key advantage:** Win one brand = potential fleet-wide deployment

## Revenue Model (Triple Play)

### 1. SaaS Revenue (Predictable)
**Cruise lines pay for the platform**

- **Pricing:** $10,000 - $50,000 per ship per year
- **Positioning:** Guest experience enhancement + cost savings
- **Market potential:** 300 ships × $30k avg = **$9M ARR**

**Value prop to cruise lines:**
- Reduce guest services workload 40-60%
- Increase guest satisfaction scores
- Multi-language support (40+ languages)
- Real-time updates for port changes

---

### 2. Affiliate Revenue (Explosive)
**Tour operators pay commission per booking**

**How it works:**
- AI recommends curated local experiences
- Guest books through affiliate link
- We earn 10-20% commission
- Revenue split with cruise line (50/50)

**Example math (per ship):**
- 3,000 passengers per week
- 30% book recommended activities = 900 bookings
- Average booking value: $100
- Commission rate: 15%
- **Weekly revenue:** $13,500
- **Annual revenue per ship:** $700,000

**At scale:** 100 ships × $700k = **$70M annual affiliate revenue**

---

### 3. Premium Placements (Future)
**Tour operators bid for featured spots**

- "Sponsored experiences" in AI recommendations
- Pay-per-click or pay-per-impression model
- Tourism board partnerships ("Official Barcelona Partner")
- Estimated: $50-200k per major port annually
